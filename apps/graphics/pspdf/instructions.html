Instructions

Well, I don't know anyone that wouldn't understand how to use PSPDF, but I will explain anyway just in case there is someone having trouble with it...

**Note: You will need an internet connection for this.**

1: To start viewing your PDF's, just click "PSPDF".

2: Now it will take you to the PSPDF screen. All you have to do is select your PDF using the "Select" button.

3: After you're done picking your PDF to view, select if you want the PDF format in either "HTML" or "Text". **If you select HTML, it will come out like a webpage. If you select Text, you can save it in text format (txt) to your memory stick, to view on the go, or if it's a big file and you would like to read it while on a trip.**

4:Now that you've finished picking the PDF format, click "View" to view your PDF.

Hope you enjoy viewing your PDF's on your PSP!
